Meal Prep Sunday

Cook once, eat all week. Everything comes from the grocery list. ~2 hours, both of you, done.
The system: Prep 5 days of containers. His are bigger portions, hers are smaller. Same base meals, just scaled. Label them or use different colored containers.

The 2-Hour Prep Flow

Do this together on Sunday. Put on some music, knock it out.

0:00 — Start the Rice
Rinse 4 cups jasmine rice, add to rice cooker with water. Set it and forget it. This makes enough for both of you all week.
0:05 — Season & Bake Chicken
3 lbs chicken breast + 2 lbs thighs. Season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper, olive oil. Bake at 400°F for 25 min. Breasts for her (leaner), thighs for him (more flavor/fat).
0:10 — Sweet Potatoes In
Cube 5 sweet potatoes, toss with olive oil + salt. Spread on a sheet pan, into the oven alongside chicken. Same temp, same time.
0:15 — Brown the Turkey
2 lbs ground turkey in a large skillet. Season with cumin, chili powder, garlic, onion powder. This becomes taco bowls for 3-4 days.
0:25 — Cook Eggs
Hard-boil a dozen eggs. Easy grab-and-go protein. 6 min boil → ice bath → peel → container.
0:30 — Prep Veggies
Steam 2 bags of broccoli. Wash and bag spinach for salads. Slice avocados day-of (they brown). Portion berries into snack bags.
0:45 — Make Overnight Oats (5 jars)
Each jar: ½ cup oats + ¾ cup oat milk (him) or almond milk (her) + 1 scoop protein + berries. Fridge. Grab and eat for breakfast/break-fast.
1:00 — Everything's Done Cooking
Pull chicken, sweet potatoes out. Let cool 10 min. Slice chicken into strips.
1:15 — Container Assembly
Build the containers (see portions below). 5 lunch containers + 5 dinner containers each = 20 total.
1:45 — Clean Up & Done
Wash everything. Stack containers in fridge. You're set for the week.

The 5 Meals You're Rotating

Both
1. Chicken & Rice Bowl
Simple. Effective. The bodybuilder classic.
His: 8 oz chicken thigh + 1.5 cups rice + 1 cup broccoli + hot sauce
Hers: 5 oz chicken breast + 1 cup rice + 1 cup broccoli + squeeze of lime
Both
2. Turkey Taco Bowl
Seasoned ground turkey over rice with all the fixings.
His: 8 oz turkey + 1.5 cups rice + black beans + salsa + ½ avocado
Hers: 5 oz turkey + 1 cup rice + black beans + salsa + ¼ avocado + Greek yogurt (sour cream sub)
Both
3. Sweet Potato & Salmon Plate
Omega-3 powerhouse. Great for recovery and skin health.
His: 8 oz salmon fillet + 1 large sweet potato + spinach salad + olive oil drizzle
Hers: 5 oz salmon fillet + 1 medium sweet potato + spinach salad + lemon squeeze
Both
4. Egg & Oat Power Breakfast
Break-fast meal (noon for him) or morning fuel (for her).
His: 4 eggs scrambled + overnight oats jar + ½ avocado + hot sauce
Hers: 3 eggs scrambled + overnight oats jar + berries
Both
5. PB Protein Wrap
Quick grab when you don't feel like reheating. 3 min, no microwave needed.
His: 2 whole wheat tortillas + 3 tbsp PB + banana slices + honey drizzle
Hers: 1 tortilla + 2 tbsp PB + banana slices + berries

Grab-and-Go Snacks

Pre-portioned during Sunday prep. Keep these in the fridge for between meals.

His Snacks
• 2 hard-boiled eggs
• Greek yogurt + honey
• Rice cake + PB + banana
• Handful of almonds
• Protein shake (Vega)
Her Snacks
• 1 hard-boiled egg + fruit
• Greek yogurt + berries
• Rice cake + PB
• Apple + almonds
• Protein shake (Whey)

Storage Tips

Fridge life: Cooked chicken/turkey = 4 days. Rice = 5 days. Hard-boiled eggs = 5 days. Overnight oats = 4 days. Prep Sunday, eat through Thursday, Friday = fresh cook or eat out night. Sweet potatoes = 5 days. Salmon = 3 days (eat those containers first).
Containers: Get a 20-pack of glass meal prep containers from Aldi or Amazon. Glass > plastic (no microwave chemicals, easier to clean). Label with masking tape: "His - Lunch" / "Hers - Dinner" etc.
