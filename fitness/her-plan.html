Her Solo Plan

3-day bodyweight starter — shape, strength, and core. No equipment needed to start.

Warm-Up (Every Session)

  • March in place (1 min)
  • Arm circles (30 sec each direction)
  • Hip circles (10 each direction)
  • Bodyweight squats (10 slow reps)
  • Cat-cow stretches (10 reps)

Day 1 — Lower Body & Posture

Monday or Tuesday
Bodyweight Squats Watch →
3 × 15. Feet shoulder-width, sit back like a chair. Knees track over toes.
Glute Bridges Watch →
3 × 15. Lie on back, feet flat, push hips up. Squeeze glutes 2 sec at top.
Reverse Lunges Watch →
3 × 10 each leg. Step back, both knees 90°. Keep torso upright.
Wall Angels Watch →
3 × 10. Back flat against wall, arms slide up and down. Great for desk posture.
Calf Raises Watch →
3 × 15. Rise up on toes, hold 1 sec at top. Use a step for extra range.

Day 2 — Full Body Toning

Wednesday or Thursday
Push-Ups (or Knee Push-Ups) Watch →
3 × 8-12. Hands shoulder-width, body straight line. Drop to knees if needed — no shame.
Sumo Squats Watch →
3 × 15. Wide stance, toes pointed out. Targets inner thighs + glutes.
Tricep Dips (Chair) Watch →
3 × 10. Hands on edge of sturdy chair, lower until elbows at 90°.
Side-Lying Leg Raises Watch →
3 × 12 each side. Lie on side, lift top leg slowly. Keep hips stacked.
Superman Hold Watch →
3 × 10 (hold 3 sec each). Lie face down, lift arms + legs off floor.

Day 3 — Strength & Core

Friday or Saturday
Single-Leg Glute Bridges Watch →
3 × 10 each leg. One foot up, drive through planted heel. Level up from Day 1.
Step-Ups (Stairs or Bench) Watch →
3 × 10 each leg. Drive through the top foot, don't push off the bottom.
Plank Hold Watch →
3 × 20-30 sec. Forearms on floor, body straight. Squeeze everything.
Dead Bugs Watch →
3 × 10 each side. Back pressed into floor, opposite arm + leg extend.
Bird Dogs Watch →
3 × 10 each side. On all fours, extend opposite arm + leg. Hold 2 sec.

Key Tips

Form over weight, always. It's better to do 8 perfect reps than 15 sloppy ones. Record yourself to check form — you'd be surprised what you see.
Protein matters. Hit 100-120g daily. Whey shake post-workout is the easiest win. See the Supplement Guide.
Consistency > perfection. 3 days a week, every week. Miss a day? Just pick up where you left off. Never restart from zero.
