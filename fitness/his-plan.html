His Plan

Full body every session — upper, lower, and core mixed together so nothing gets destroyed. 3-day base, optional 4th + pool day. No fixed weekdays, just A → B → C in order.

Realistic timeline: 14-18 months. Loose skin is expected — muscle building + slow loss rate is the #1 strategy to minimize it.

Why Full Body 3x/Week?

Every muscle, every session. Schoenfeld's meta-analysis: training each muscle 2-3x/week is optimal for hypertrophy. Full body 3x hits everything 3x/week. Lower volume per muscle per session = less soreness, better recovery. At 285, more muscle groups per session also means higher calorie burn per workout.

Cardio Protocol

Fat Loss Without Muscle Loss
Cardio supports the deficit — it doesn't create it. The deficit comes from diet. Cardio is for heart health, recovery, and a small caloric bonus.
10,000 Steps Daily
Non-negotiable baseline. NEAT accounts for more daily calories than any gym session. This alone burns 300-500 extra calories.
15-20 Min Incline Treadmill Walk — After Every Lifting Session
3.0-3.5 mph, 10-12% incline. Low-impact, burns fat without cortisol spike. Do this immediately after lifting when glycogen is depleted.
Pool Sessions — Best Cardio at Your Weight
1-2x per week, 60 min. Burns 400-700 cal with zero joint stress. Way better than running at 285 lbs.
HIIT — Bike or Rower Only
Optional 1x per week. 20-30 sec all-out, 60-90 sec easy, 15-20 min total. NO running at 285 lbs.

Skin Management Strategy

The Real Talk — Research-Backed
At 285, significant loose skin is expected. But the research is clear: slow loss rate + muscle building + targeted nutrition gives skin the best chance to retract.

1. Slow Loss Rate — 1.5 lbs/Week

Faster loss = skin can't remodel fast enough. The 700-800 cal deficit hits this rate at your weight.

2. Muscle Building — Your #1 Weapon

Muscle fills the space that fat leaves. Every pound of muscle = less "empty" loose skin.

3. Skin Health Supplements

  • Collagen peptides: 10-15g/day — improved skin elasticity after 8+ weeks
  • Vitamin C: 500-1000mg/day — required for collagen synthesis
  • Zinc: 15-30mg/day — skin cell turnover and repair
  • Omega-3s: 2-3g EPA/DHA daily — supports skin barrier

4. Hydration

1 gallon (128 oz) per day minimum. Skin elasticity is directly linked to hydration status.

5. Surgery Timeline — Be Patient

Most surgeons recommend maintaining goal weight for 12-24 months before assessing. Skin continues to retract for up to 2 years.

  • Lower abdomen: Very likely needs surgery. Least elastic recoil.
  • Chest: Depends on muscle built underneath.
  • Arms & thighs: May tighten naturally with enough muscle mass and time.

Common Mistakes Big Guys Make

Mistake #1
Too Much Cardio, Not Enough Lifting
Cardio burns calories but doesn't build muscle. Lifting is the main course, cardio is the side dish.
Mistake #2
Cutting Calories Too Aggressively
Going below 2,000 at your size tanks metabolism and kills testosterone. 700-800 cal deficit is aggressive enough.
Mistake #3
Not Tracking Progressive Overload
Write down every set, every rep, every weight. If last week was 135x6, this week should be 135x7 or 140x6.
Mistake #4
Avoiding Heavy Compound Lifts
Squats, deadlifts, bench, and rows build the most muscle per minute. Compound lifts release more testosterone and growth hormone.
Mistake #5
Scale Obsession
The scale lies during recomp. Track: waist measurement (weekly), progress photos (biweekly), strength numbers (every session).
